# Salesforce & Blackthorn Integration Strategy

Business Analysis Case Study | CRM Workflow & Reporting Design

This project presents a conceptual Salesforce + Blackthorn integration strategy designed to support scalable university event lifecycle management. The solution focuses on centralizing event registrations, attendance tracking, payment workflows, feedback collection, and executive reporting within a unified CRM-driven ecosystem.

## Business Problem

University engagement teams often manage event operations across disconnected workflows, making it difficult to maintain visibility into registrations, attendance, payments, feedback, and program performance. These fragmented processes create operational inefficiencies, inconsistent reporting, and limited decision-making visibility for leadership teams.

## Proposed Solution

The proposed solution introduces a two-layer architecture:

* **Blackthorn Operational Layer** for event creation, registration management, attendance tracking, payment handling, and operational workflows.
* **Salesforce CRM Engagement & Reporting Layer** for campaign management, engagement visibility, automation, feedback capture, and KPI reporting.

## Key Features

* End-to-end event lifecycle workflow mapping
* ERD/data modeling across Salesforce and Blackthorn objects
* Registration and attendance tracking framework
* Payment processing and retry workflow concepts
* CRM engagement tracking and campaign integration
* Automated communication and escalation workflows
* Executive KPI dashboard strategy
* Risk mitigation and data governance considerations

## Business Impact

The conceptual framework was designed to:

* Improve operational scalability
* Reduce manual administrative effort
* Enhance student engagement visibility
* Strengthen data integrity and governance
* Deliver executive-ready reporting and KPI insights
* Support data-driven planning and decision-making
